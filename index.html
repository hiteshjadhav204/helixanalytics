Meet the founder

Hitesh Jadhav - Founder

Mr. Hitesh Jadhav is the Founder of Helix Analytics, a certified Data Analyst with a strong foundation in both technical and analytical disciplines. He holds a Bachelor's Degree in Mechanical Engineering, a stream known for its depth in mathematics and problem solving. This technical background has shaped his structured thinking, logical reasoning, and ability to approach problems methodically, qualities that align with data analytics. After graduation, Mr. Jadhav worked in financial markets for over three years, gaining hands-on experience with technical and fundamental analysis and building an understanding of market behavior and data interpretation. Later, driven by his passion for technology and data, he transitioned into Business and Data Analytics, completing certifications and hands-on projects in Python, SQL, Power BI, Tableau, Excel, and statistical analysis. His approach combines technical expertise with curiosity and discipline, focusing on clarity, accuracy, and meaningful outcomes in every analysis.
